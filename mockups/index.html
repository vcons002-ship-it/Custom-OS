Legend — the Weave's zones

Stage — the content in focus, center.  ·  Tool Halo — materialized Surfaces orbiting it.  ·  Foresight Rail — related content & next actions (violet).  ·  Curiosity — questions, never guesses (amber).  ·  Cloud — anything leaving the device is disclosed (sky).  ·  ● the presence indicator — the mind, breathing.
